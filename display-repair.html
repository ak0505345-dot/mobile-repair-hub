📺 Display Repair Guide

Samsung Galaxy A15

Tools Required

Step-by-Step Repair

  1. Power off the phone.
  2. Remove the SIM tray.
  3. Heat the back cover.
  4. Open the back cover carefully.
  5. Disconnect the battery.
  6. Remove the damaged display.
  7. Install the new display.
  8. Reconnect the battery.
  9. Test the display.
  10. Close the phone.
